Summary

  • I am a self-taught front-end web developer and UI designer, based in Brussels (Belgium).
  • I am highly proficient in the technologies that are at the core of front-end development : HTML, CSS and Javascript. I believe very strong knowledge of these foundations is essential to being a good front-end developer, and everything else (frameworks in particular) can be learned as needed.
  • I believe coding standards and best practices are crucial to the long-term success of a digital product. I am not a "code ninja". If anything, I like to think of myself as a code janitor.
