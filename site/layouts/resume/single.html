About me

I'm a front-end web developer and UI designer with a focus on performance and accessibility, based in Brussels (Belgium). I'm highly proficient in the technologies that are at the core of front-end development : HTML, CSS and Javascript. I believe that very strong knowledge of these foundations is essential to being a good front-end developer, and everything else (frameworks in particular) can be learned as needed.

I believe that a good user interface is like a joke : you shouldn't have to explain it. I value simplicity and intuitiveness over complex and overly engineered solutions. I love working with prototypes to validate hypothesis and iterate rapidly, as well as design systems to build consistent UI at scale.

I see open communication, empathy and inclusivity as the essential components of a well-functioning team. For more information about myself, I invite you to have a look at my about page : {{ .Site.BaseURL }}about.
