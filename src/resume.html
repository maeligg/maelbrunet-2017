Summary

  • I am a self-taught front-end web developer and UI designer, based in Brussels (Belgium).
  • I believe coding standards and best practices are crucial for the long-term success of a digital product. I am not a "code ninja". If anything, I am a code janitor.
  • I believe that a good user interface is like a joke : you shouldn't have to explain it.
  • I enjoy making website for real humans, not for Awwwards or to show off whatever new tech is currently in vogue.
  • I see teamwork, open communication and empathy as essential to working as a group effectively, and delivering quality.
  • I am highly proficient in the technologies that are at the core of front-end development : HTML, CSS and Javascript. I believe very strong knowledge of these foundations is essential to being a good front-end developer, and everything else (frameworks in particular) can be learned as needed.
