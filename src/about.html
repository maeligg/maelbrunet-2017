My "origin story"

I first dipped my toes in web development by accident, when I accepted to create a new prototype website for my employer at the time. Of course I was way over my head and I had no idea what I was doing, but I managed to create a basic Wordpress site that is still online (don't judge me too harshly), and made me fall in love with web development. I took some time afterwards to properly learn the fundamentals on my own, and then I applied and started to work in a digital agency.

Outside of web development

I love to run, I find it physically satisfying and I like pushing myself to improve on a regular basis.

Food is very important to me, I enjoy every part of the process : picking the right ingredients, cooking (I'm not big into cakes, but I like making just about anything else), and sharing a meal in good company. I find the whole process suprisingly similar to web development.

I play video games (though not as much as I used to), mostly on PC, and I have a particular fondness for rich narrative experiences and emergent stories.

Other than that, I enjoy spending time with friends, preferably over a nice, cool beer.
